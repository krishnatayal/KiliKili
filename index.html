Mahishmati Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning(MPTEL)




Kya hai mrityu, hamare aatmabal se shatru ka bal jyada hai, ye sochna hai mrityu. Ranbhumi mein shatru se bhaybheet hokar jivit rehna hai mrityu. Jis neech ne hamari maa ka apman kia hai, vo hamare samne attahas kar raha hai, uska sar katkar maa ke charno mein arpit karne ki jagah, peeth dikha kar bhagna hai mrityu. Us mrityu ko maarne jaa raha hun main. Meri maa aurr matrabhumi ko koi chhu nahi sakta, uski chhati chirkar, unsab ko ye kehne ja raha hun main.

Mere sath aayega kaun!! Mere sath ladega kaun!!
